On-Going Community Projects

Women Empowerment Graduation

Oasis of Hope Health Center's initiative in Bulapesa is indeed remarkable, exemplifying Lokho Karayu's compassionate leadership and the center's dedication to community empowerment. Through their focus on providing essential life skills, savings programs, and financial start-up kits, they are not only uplifting individual women but also fortifying the entire community.
The palpable anticipation among the women of Bulapesa speaks volumes about the project's potential to elevate their living standards and improve family dynamics. Moreover, the center's holistic approach, extending support to the youth for establishing profitable projects, showcases a keen understanding of community needs and a commitment to sustainable development.
Initiatives like these are more than just programs; they serve as guiding lights, illuminating pathways for individuals and communities to thrive despite adversities. The Oasis of Hope Health Center stands as a beacon of hope and progress in Bulapesa, inspiring others to join in the effort to uplift marginalized communities.

The significance of February's 2024 milestone cannot be overstated. The graduation of 150 women symbolizes not only their personal achievements but also the effectiveness of the center's initiatives and the dedication of its team. It marks a pivotal moment in these women's lives, propelling them towards economic independence and a brighter future for themselves and their families. This success is a testament to the transformative power of empowerment and community support.

Youth and Women Empowerment Project

Oasis of Hope Health center stands as a beacon of empowerment, actively engaged in an ongoing initiative to support over 150 women in Bulapesa. Under the dedicated guidance of Lokho Karayu, a compassionate social worker leading the project, the center is committed to enhancing the lives of these women through imparting essential life skills, facilitating savings programs, and providing financial start-up kits.
The women of Bulapesa are eagerly anticipating the positive impact of this empowerment project, which seeks to elevate their standards of living and enrich their family lives. Beyond its focus on women, Oasis of Hope Health center extends its benevolent reach to the youth of Bulapesa. The center takes proactive steps to assist them in establishing projects that are not only easily manageable but also inherently profitable. This holistic approach reflects the center's dedication to fostering positive transformations in both the lives of women and the youth of the Bulapesa region.

Community Home Visit

Oasis of Hope Health center goes beyond providing outpatient services, extending its care to encompass community home visits in the Bulapesa area. These visits are dedicated to aiding individuals who are unable to access the health facility independently. In addition to addressing immediate health needs, these home visits serve as a platform for medical practitioners to emphasize the significance of having a nearby health facility.
Health education stands as a cornerstone initiative for Oasis of Hope Health center. By actively engaging in teaching the local community about the paramount importance of personal health, the center aims to not only diminish the incidence of preventable deaths but also dispel myths and misconceptions associated with modern medicine. Recognizing the vital role of health education, the center endeavors to empower the local community with a deeper understanding of the essential nature of health and well-being.

Medical Camp 2023

Medical Diagnosis and Treatment

In 2023, the Oasis of Hope Health center conducted two medical camps, the first in January and the second in July. The January camp spanned three days, providing attendees who participated for three consecutive days with complimentary medical diagnosis and treatment. This initiative greatly benefited over 1200 individuals facing financial challenges in accessing healthcare. The medical camps have proven instrumental in assisting the people of Bulapesa, contributing significantly to saving lives in the region. A heartfelt appreciation goes to the Oasis of Hope Health center for their invaluable contributions.

Dental Care

The Dental Care Medical Camp at the Oasis of Hope Health center has played a crucial role in educating the people of Bula about the significance of dental care. Over 500 individuals have received complimentary dental care services, contributing to a notable decrease in dental cases in the Bulapesa region. This initiative has been particularly effective in reducing reliance on traditional and outdated dental care practices. Given the strong cultural roots in Bulapesa, where people traditionally used sticks for teeth cleaning, the dental care awareness campaign has successfully conveyed the importance of embracing modern dental care methods.

Youth Training On Sex Education, Drugs & Substance Abuse and Career

Taking the lead in uniting the youth of Bulapesa, Oasis of Hope Health center has played a crucial role in educating them on the importance of sex education, the perils of drug and substance abuse, and the significance of career choices. By imparting these vital lessons, the center aspires to positively shape the future leaders of Bulapesa, recognizing the pivotal role that youths play in the community's development.
Oasis of Hope Health center is committed to fostering an understanding of the potential dangers associated with drug use among the youth, allowing them to redirect their focus entirely towards education. This proactive approach contributes to a significant reduction in the number of drug users in Bulapesa, fostering a more vibrant and drug-free community.
Furthermore, the center is dedicated to enlightening the youth about various career paths, emphasizing the transformative impact a fulfilling career can have on their lives. Encouraging an open-minded approach, Oasis of Hope Health center strives to help the youth realize their potential and comprehend how their individual contributions can bring about positive change in their community.

The Start Of The Water Project

Oasis of Hope Health center has undertaken the commendable initiative of establishing a borehole to enhance water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) conditions. Collaborating with Isiolo Water & Sewerage Company (IWASCO), the center is committed to efficiently distributing water to the neighboring households. This strategic partnership aims to promote proper sanitation within the local community, effectively addressing the risk of diseases such as cholera and typhoid. The borehole project is instrumental in ensuring that residents in the Bulapesa area have access to clean and safe water for both consumption and general hygiene practices.

In conclusion, Oasis of Hope Health center emerges as a transformative force in the Bulapesa community, extending its impact far beyond conventional healthcare services. Through initiatives like empowering women, educating the youth on critical issues, and investing in water infrastructure, the center demonstrates a holistic commitment to the well-being of the community. By addressing essential aspects such as health, education, and access to clean water, Oasis of Hope Health center not only strives to improve immediate living conditions but also lays the groundwork for a healthier, more resilient future for the residents of Bulapesa. As a beacon of positive change, the center exemplifies the profound impact that community-focused initiatives can have on enhancing the overall quality of life.
