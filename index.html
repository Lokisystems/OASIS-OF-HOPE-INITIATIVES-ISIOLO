About Us

Oasis of Health center commenced its journey at the core of Isiolo County, nestled in the serene village of Bulapesa, merely 1.8 kilometers from Isiolo town. With a dual focus on providing enhanced health services and empowerment for vulnerable women and youths within the local community, the center was founded. Oasis of Hope Health center stands as a beacon of comprehensive care, striving to uplift and serve the health needs of the community. It is fully equipped with state-of-the-art, world-class medical equipment and boasts a proficient team of medical practitioners, ensuring the delivery of exceptional healthcare services while concurrently addressing the empowerment of vulnerable women and youths in the region.
