About Us

Empowerment & Healthcare Hub

Founded in 2018, Oasis of Hope Health center, located in the serene village of Bulapesa in Isiolo County, has emerged as a powerful force of positive change within the community. Its journey began as an Empowerment Center, dedicated to uplifting vulnerable women and youth, enabling them to become self-reliant and improve their standards of living. As time passed, it recognized a pressing need to evolve into a comprehensive health facility, combining empowerment and healthcare to serve the local population holistically.

Our Dedicated Team:

The Oasis of Hope Health Center houses a compassionate and skilled team of professionals, including a family physician, nurse, clinical officer, lab technician, social worker, receptionist, and security personnel. These dedicated individuals are the driving force behind the center's mission to promote health and empowerment.

Core Initiatives:

The center's commitment extends across a diverse spectrum of initiatives, reflecting its unwavering dedication to community well-being:

1. Healthcare Services:

The health center provides comprehensive medical services, ensuring that the community's healthcare needs are met by a family physician, nurse, and clinical officer.

2. Empowerment Projects:

Building on its roots as an empowerment center, Oasis of Hope continues to empower women and youth through skill-building, savings programs, and startup kits. This fosters self-reliance and raises the living standards of the local community.

3. Youth Training:

The center conducts youth education programs on crucial topics such as sex education and the dangers of substance abuse. These programs aim to equip young individuals with knowledge for responsible decision-making.

4. Career Development:

Recognizing the importance of education and career opportunities, the center provides guidance and resources to the local youth, helping them explore diverse career pathways.

5. Community Engagement:

The center goes beyond its premises by conducting home visits, ensuring that even those unable to reach the center receive the care and support they need.

Our Mission:

The Oasis of Hope Health Center's mission is rooted in capacity-building and community empowerment. It strives to achieve this through empowerment training, microfinance platforms, startup kits, nutritional programs, and community health initiatives. By implementing these multifaceted programs, the center aims to improve the well-being and self-sufficiency of the local community.

Areas of Intervention:

The center's areas of intervention encompass a wide array of community-focused initiatives, including community service, women and youth empowerment, mentorship, and training of young individuals, water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) initiatives, and community health and nutrition programs.

Our Core Values:

At the heart of the Oasis of Hope Health Center are core values that guide its every action:

- Prayer:

A foundation of faith that brings strength and unity.

- Love:

An unwavering commitment to compassion and care for all.

- Hospitality:

Welcoming all with open arms and creating a nurturing environment.

- Excellence:

Striving for the highest standards in all aspects of service.

- Integrity:

Upholding honesty and ethical conduct in every endeavor.

The Oasis of Hope Health Center is not just a healthcare facility; it is a sanctuary for empowerment, love, and well-being in the heart of Bulapesa. It continues to inspire and uplift the community, symbolizing hope and a brighter future for all who pass through its doors.
